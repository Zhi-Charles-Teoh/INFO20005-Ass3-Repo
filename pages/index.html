Great Guitars in Queensland, Australia is home to one of the finest collections of vintage acoustic, electric, and bass guitars nationwide – not to mention our selection of amps, stomp-boxes, and guitar accessories. We also specialise in Genuine Hofner guitar part replacement, some hand made parts and specialist repairs and restorations.

Browse our online gallery today and grab yourself a piece of rock’n’roll history while you can! You’re sure to find that perfect bit of rare and collectible gig gear here at Great Guitars.

Based on the Coral Coast in Wide Bay, our store is a veritable treasure trove of rare beauties, hidden gems, and long-lost classic axes from a number of leading guitar manufacturers: Fender, Gibson, Gretsch, Martin, Maton, Ibanez and Hofner are just some of the names we stock and specialise in. These can be current classics or relics of yesteryear at retail prices – one-of-a-kind, sonic delights of unparalleled craftsmanship.
